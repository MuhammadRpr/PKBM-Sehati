# Product Requirements Document: PKBM SEHATI - Community Learning Center Website

## Problem Statement

Many individuals in the Takari area of Kupang, Indonesia, lack access to formal education and are unaware of non-formal educational opportunities. Traditional educational institutions often fail to cater to the diverse needs of the community, particularly for those who have dropped out of school or are looking to continue their education at a flexible pace. PKBM SEHATI aims to address this gap by providing a user-friendly, informative platform that introduces and promotes non-formal education programs.

## Goals & Success Metrics

### Goals

1. **Increase Awareness**: Raise awareness about non-formal educational programs among the target audience.
2. **Drive Enrollment**: Facilitate the enrollment process for potential students.
3. **Provide Valuable Information**: Serve as a comprehensive resource for information about non-formal education, including curriculum details, program offerings, and community news.

### Success Metrics

1. **Website Traffic**: Increase monthly visitors by 50% within the first year.
2. **Enrollment Rates**: Achieve a 20% increase in new student enrollments within the first six months.
3. **User Engagement**: Ensure an average session duration of at least 2 minutes and a bounce rate below 40%.
4. **Feedback**: Collect positive feedback from at least 70% of users regarding the website's usability and information quality.

## User Stories

1. **As a potential student**, I want to easily understand the programs offered by PKBM SEHATI so that I can decide if they meet my educational needs.
2. **As a parent**, I want to quickly find information about the curriculum and enrollment process so that I can support my child's education.
3. **As a community member**, I want to stay updated with the latest news and activities of PKBM SEHATI so that I can engage with the center's initiatives.
4. **As an administrator**, I want to easily update the website content so that it remains current and relevant.

## Functional Requirements

1. **Hero Section**
   - **Headline**: "Pendidikan untuk Semua, Tanpa Batas Usia"
   - **Subtext**: PKBM SEHATI hadir sebagai solusi pendidikan kesetaraan di Takari
   - **CTA Buttons**: "Daftar Sekarang" and "Lihat Program"

2. **Kurikulum Section**
   - Detailed explanation of the non-formal education curriculum (Paket A, B, C) as per the Ministry of Education.
   - Highlight key features such as flexibility, practicality, and community-focused content.

3. **Program Section**
   - Overview of the main programs: Paket A (SD), Paket B (SMP), Paket C (SMA).
   - Optional sections on supplementary programs: life skills, basic entrepreneurship.

4. **Artikel Section**
   - Blog-like section featuring news about PKBM activities, educational tips, and the benefits of non-formal education.
   - Admin-friendly CMS to enable easy content updates.

5. **Kontak Section**
   - School address, email, and Google Maps integration.
   - Simple contact form for inquiries.
   - Optional integration with WhatsApp for quick communication.

6. **Mobile-Friendly Design**
   - Responsive layout that adapts to various screen sizes.
   - Optimized for fast loading times, especially on mobile devices with limited internet connectivity.

7. **Clear Navigation**
   - Simple and intuitive navigation menu.
   - Scroll-based landing page design for easy browsing.

8. **CTA Visibility**
   - Prominent "Daftar Sekarang" and "Kontak" buttons throughout the website to encourage user actions.

## Non-Functional Requirements

1. **Performance**
   - Load time: Less than 3 seconds.
   - Mobile-first design optimization.
   - SEO-friendly structure to improve search engine rankings.

2. **Usability**
   - Easy-to-read typography.
   - Clear and concise language.
   - Consistent and intuitive user interface.

3. **Security**
   - HTTPS encryption for secure browsing.
   - Regular backups and data protection measures.

4. **Maintainability**
   - Admin-friendly CMS for content management.
   - Documentation and training materials for website administrators.
